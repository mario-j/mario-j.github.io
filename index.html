While full stack web and mobile development has been my primary focus, I'm interested in expanding my knowledge in other facets of the computer science world, too. Ranging from building Python web scrapers, training machine learning models, and design prototyping to name a few.

Outside of my life as a developer my days are often filled with playing piano, watching sports, and binging Tiny Desk Concerts.
